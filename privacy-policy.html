                                    PRIVACY POLICY FOR USERS

                                    STAYOH is a Guest Engagement Application which helps hotels, properties, and other businesses
                                    (collectively, “Properties”) deliver better service by helping staff communicates with each other and
                                    with their guests. As part of the user agreement entered with the properties, this Mobile Application is
                                    made available for download by the Guest of the properties and for avail the in-house services of the
                                    Properties.

                                    We want Property guests and staff to stay in control of their data. We do not sell guest data or use it to
                                    contact people for marketing purposes. We commit to strong privacy protection principles, as outlined
                                    in this privacy policy (“Policy”).

                                    We respect the privacy rights of users and recognise the importance of protecting and handling
                                    information collected about you in accordance with both the law and best practice. The Privacy Policy
                                    tells you how your information (including personal data and/or sensitive personal data) is collected,
                                    used, stored and disclosed by us as well as specific choices you can make about your information. 

                                    Installation of this Mobile Application would render your acceptance to the Privacy Policy as
                                    mentioned below. If you do not agree to these terms of this Privacy Policy, we request you to
                                    immediately uninstall the Application and discontinue using our services. 

                                    Terms of the Privacy Policy will come into force immediately on successful installation of the mobile
                                    application.

                                    We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will
                                    alert you about any changes by updating the “Last updated” date of this Privacy Policy. You are
                                    encouraged to periodically review this Privacy Policy to stay informed of updates. You will be
                                    deemed to have been made aware of, will be subject to, and will be deemed to have accepted the

                                    revised Privacy Policy is posted.
